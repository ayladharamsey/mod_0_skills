Class: Entrees

Objects: Chicken Parmesan (string), Caeser Salad (string), Hamburger (string)

Attributes: Entre_name (string), Entre_price (integer or float), Entre_on_happy_hour (boolean), Entre_ingredients (string)

Methods: Order_entree (Employee orders entree in system for customer untilizing Entre_name)

Customize_entree (Customer orders an entree, but would like it to be customized. Utilizes attribute Entre_name and Entre_ingredient.)

Apply_happy_hour_discount(Employee runs this method if the customer is purchasing food or drink applicable to happy hour utilizing the Entre_on_happy_hour attribute)
